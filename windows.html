We use a combination of Waterfed Poles and traditional window cleaning techniques to ensure that your windows are sparkling clean when we pack up and leave!

A Waterfed pole is just what it sounds like! The highest quality filtered water flows through the tubing inside the poles and comes out directly from our scrubbing instruments at your window. This allows our cleaners to reach your second and third story windows without the use of a ladder without sacrificing quality!

Our Waterfed system connects to any outdoor spigot and purifies your water through a three-step filtration process before feeding the water up to our poles. This not only allows our employees to stand firmly on the ground without having to use a ladder, it also removes any need to squeegee each exterior window as the pure water dries completely clear without the use of any chemicals!

No ladders, no squeegees, no chemicals, and no streaks! Using waterfed poles leads to quicker completion time and allows us to clean your frames while simultaneously cleaning your glass.
